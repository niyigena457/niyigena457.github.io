Electronic commerce

Rwanda

Electronic commerce (e-commerce) in Rwanda has seen significant growth over recent years. The government has prioritized digital transformation, aiming to make Rwanda a tech hub in Africa. Increased internet penetration and smartphone usage have facilitated this growth. Key sectors benefiting from e-commerce include retail, agriculture, and services. Platforms like Irembo and marketplaces such as Jumia Rwanda are popular among consumers. Mobile money services like M-Pesa and MTN Mobile Money have also boosted online transactions. Challenges remain, such as logistics, delivery infrastructure, and limited digital literacy. However, ongoing investments and supportive policies promise a bright future for e-commerce in Rwanda.
