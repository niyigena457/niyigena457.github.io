Rwandan Elections

Electronic commerce

Rwanda's recent elections have drawn significant attention both domestically and internationally. Held on July 15, 2024, these elections saw a high voter turnout, with citizens eager to participate in the democratic process. Incumbent President Paul Kagame, who has been in power since 2000, was a prominent candidate. His party, the Rwandan Patriotic Front (RPF), has maintained a strong grip on the political landscape of Rwanda for decades.
